  • Poverty Point Objects (PPOs)

    These hand-formed fired earth objects, and fragments thereof, are the most common artifact type at Poverty Point. Also known as baked clay balls, cooking balls, or PPOs, these ceramic artifacts served as replacements for stone in hot rock cooking, what was the dominant cooking technology of the time.
